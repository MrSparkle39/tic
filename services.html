Comprehensive NDIS Support Services

At The Inclusion Crew, we provide a complete range of disability support services designed to help NDIS participants live independently, engage with their community, and achieve their personal goals. Every service is delivered with professionalism, respect, and genuine care for your wellbeing.

In Home Support

Our in-home support services are designed to help you maintain independence and comfort in your own home. We understand that your home is your sanctuary, and our support workers respect your space while providing the assistance you need to live well.

What We Offer:

  • Daily Living Assistance - Help with routine tasks including dressing, bathing, grooming, and mobility support
  • Personal Care - Respectful assistance with intimate care needs, always prioritizing your dignity and preferences
  • Home Maintenance - Light housekeeping, laundry, bed making, and keeping your living space comfortable
  • Meal Preparation - Planning, preparing, and cooking nutritious meals tailored to your dietary needs and preferences
  • Companionship - Friendly support and social interaction to combat isolation and loneliness

Our in-home support workers are carefully matched to your personality and needs. They're not just providing a service - they're building a relationship based on trust, respect, and understanding. Whether you need a few hours of support each week or more comprehensive daily assistance, we're here to help you live your best life at home.

Personal Care

Support for grooming, hygiene, and health needs, ensuring individual well-being with precision and care.

Personal care is one of the most intimate services we provide, and we approach it with the utmost professionalism, sensitivity, and respect for your dignity. Our trained support workers understand the importance of maintaining your independence while providing the assistance you need.

Our Personal Care Services Include:

  • Bathing and Showering - Safe assistance with bathing, including transfer support and adaptive equipment use
  • Grooming and Hygiene - Help with brushing teeth, hair care, shaving, nail care, and skincare routines
  • Dressing and Undressing - Assistance with clothing selection and getting dressed comfortably
  • Toileting Support - Respectful assistance with bathroom needs and continence management
  • Mobility Assistance - Help with transfers, positioning, and moving safely around your home

We recognize that accepting help with personal care can feel vulnerable. That's why we prioritize building trust and rapport with our participants. Our support workers receive comprehensive training in person-centered care, infection control, manual handling, and maintaining dignity in care. You're always in control - we follow your preferences, respect your privacy, and adapt our approach to suit your comfort level.

Community Participation

Tailored assistance for active involvement in local events, social gatherings, and recreational activities.

Being part of your community is essential for wellbeing, social connection, and quality of life. Our community participation support helps you engage in activities you enjoy, try new experiences, develop skills, and build meaningful relationships.

Community Activities We Support:

  • Social Events - Attend parties, gatherings, community events, and celebrations with support
  • Recreational Activities - Participate in sports, fitness classes, art groups, music programs, and hobbies
  • Educational Opportunities - Access courses, workshops, libraries, and learning experiences
  • Cultural Activities - Visit museums, galleries, theaters, concerts, and cultural festivals
  • Volunteering - Engage in meaningful volunteer work and give back to your community
  • Religious Services - Attend places of worship and participate in faith-based activities

Our support workers don't just accompany you - they actively facilitate your participation. Whether you need help with transport, communication support, physical assistance, or just someone to share the experience with, we're there to ensure you can fully engage. We help you discover new interests, develop existing skills, and build confidence in community settings.

Mealtime Assistance

Preparation and support for eating, ensuring nutrition and comfort with practical and personalized care.

Good nutrition is fundamental to health and wellbeing. Our mealtime assistance services ensure you enjoy nutritious, delicious meals that meet your dietary needs and personal preferences. We make mealtimes enjoyable, social, and stress-free.

How We Help With Meals:

  • Meal Planning - Create weekly meal plans that incorporate your preferences, dietary requirements, and nutritional needs
  • Food Shopping - Accompany you to shops or handle grocery shopping on your behalf
  • Meal Preparation - Prepare fresh, home-cooked meals or assist you to cook independently
  • Feeding Assistance - Respectful support with eating for those who need physical assistance
  • Special Diets - Cater to specific dietary requirements including allergies, intolerances, cultural preferences, and medical diets
  • Kitchen Safety - Supervise kitchen activities to ensure safety while promoting independence

We understand that food is about more than just nutrition - it's about enjoyment, culture, and connection. Our support workers can help you maintain cooking skills, try new recipes, or simply ensure you have regular, nutritious meals. We accommodate all dietary requirements including vegetarian, vegan, halal, kosher, diabetic, and texture-modified diets.

Medication Administration

Ensuring safe and timely medication intake with straightforward, hands-on assistance for your peace of mind.

Taking medications correctly and on time is crucial for managing health conditions. Our trained support workers provide medication administration support, ensuring you receive your medications safely and as prescribed by your healthcare providers.

Medication Support Services:

  • Medication Reminders - Prompt you when it's time to take medications
  • Medication Administration - Assist with or administer medications as per your care plan and legal requirements
  • Webster Pack Management - Organize and manage medication packaging systems
  • Prescription Management - Help coordinate with pharmacies for refills and pickups
  • Medication Monitoring - Observe and report any side effects or concerns to appropriate healthcare professionals
  • Documentation - Maintain accurate records of all medications administered

All our support workers who administer medications have completed appropriate training and maintain currency in medication management. We follow strict protocols to ensure safety, including double-checking medications, maintaining proper storage, and documenting all administrations. We work closely with your healthcare team to ensure continuity of care and can communicate any concerns or changes to doctors, nurses, or pharmacists as needed.

Wellness Check

Regular calls to ensure well-being, providing a friendly check-in and support for peace of mind.

Our wellness check service provides regular contact and monitoring to ensure your safety and wellbeing, particularly if you live alone or have periods without support. These friendly check-ins give you and your family peace of mind while promoting your independence.

Wellness Check Options:

  • Phone Check-ins - Regular scheduled calls to check on your wellbeing and chat
  • Home Visits - Brief visits to check you're safe, healthy, and have everything you need
  • Safety Monitoring - Ensure your home environment is safe and identify any concerns
  • Health Monitoring - Check on general health, appetite, sleep, and mood
  • Social Connection - Provide friendly conversation and reduce feelings of isolation
  • Emergency Response - Immediate action if you're unwell or in distress

Wellness checks can be scheduled daily, several times a week, or at whatever frequency suits your needs. They're particularly valuable after hospital discharge, during illness recovery, or for participants who prefer to live independently but need regular contact. Our support workers build rapport with you over time, making these check-ins something to look forward to rather than just a safety measure.

Transportation

Reliable support for safe and convenient travel, ensuring individuals reach their destinations comfortably and confidently.

Access to reliable transportation is essential for participating in your community, attending appointments, and living independently. Our transportation support ensures you can get where you need to go safely and comfortably.

Transportation Services:

  • Medical Appointments - Transport to doctor visits, specialists, therapy sessions, and hospital appointments
  • Social Outings - Get to social events, visit friends and family, attend community activities
  • Shopping Trips - Assistance getting to shops, markets, and shopping centers
  • Education and Employment - Regular transport to courses, classes, or workplace
  • Recreational Activities - Travel to sports, hobbies, entertainment, and leisure activities
  • Public Transport Training - Learn to use buses, trains, and trams independently

Our transportation support includes assistance getting in and out of vehicles, managing mobility aids and equipment, carrying shopping, and providing companionship during travel. We can provide transport in our vehicles or accompany you using your vehicle or public transport. All our drivers hold appropriate licenses and insurance, and our vehicles are well-maintained and comfortable.

Domestic Assistance

Practical help with household tasks, promoting a tidy and comfortable living environment tailored to you.

Maintaining a clean, comfortable home environment is important for health, safety, and wellbeing. Our domestic assistance services help you keep your home tidy and organized, creating a pleasant living space while building your skills where possible.

Household Tasks We Assist With:

  • General Cleaning - Vacuuming, mopping, dusting, and keeping living areas clean
  • Kitchen Maintenance - Washing dishes, wiping benches, cleaning appliances, organizing pantry
  • Bathroom Cleaning - Keeping bathrooms hygienic and well-maintained
  • Laundry Services - Washing, drying, folding, and putting away clothes and linen
  • Bed Making - Changing bed linen and making beds
  • Decluttering and Organization - Helping organize spaces and reduce clutter
  • Waste Management - Taking out rubbish and recycling bins

We take a collaborative approach to domestic assistance, working with you rather than just doing tasks for you. Where possible, we'll support you to participate in household tasks, building skills and maintaining independence. Our support workers respect your home, follow your preferences, and can help establish routines that make household management easier.

Shopping Assistance

Tailored shopping assistance: We adapt to your preferences, whether guiding you or handling it all with care.

Shopping for groceries, household items, and personal necessities is an essential life skill and community activity. Our shopping assistance helps you participate in shopping independently or manages the task for you when needed.

How We Support Your Shopping:

  • Accompanied Shopping - Go shopping together, with support for mobility, decision-making, and carrying items
  • Shopping on Your Behalf - Complete shopping according to your list and preferences when you're unable to go
  • Budget Management - Help stick to budget and make cost-effective choices
  • Product Selection - Assist with finding items, comparing products, and reading labels
  • Online Shopping - Help navigate online shopping platforms and place orders
  • Specialty Shopping - Assist with clothes shopping, pharmacy items, hardware, or other specialty purchases

Shopping trips can be tiring or overwhelming, especially in busy stores. Our support workers help make the experience more manageable and enjoyable. We can help you build confidence in shopping independently, learn budgeting skills, make healthy food choices, and navigate different shopping environments. Whether it's weekly groceries or occasional specialty shopping, we're here to help.
